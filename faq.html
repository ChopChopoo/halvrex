Frequently asked questions

Common questions about Halvrex shoes and ordering.

What is a helically-wound carbon rail?

Instead of a flat carbon plate, the HelixRail is a full-length carbon rail wound in a slight spiral. It lets the foot rotate and pronate through the gait rather than locking it into one flat motion.

Is the Helix a race shoe?

No. The Helix is a plated daily trainer. The helically-wound rail is tuned for a soft, forgiving daily ride, not for race-day forward snap.

Can I buy the Helix now?

Not in wide release yet. The first production run is going to a small group of testers and a waitlist while we gather real-world mileage.

How many miles will the Helix last?

We are still gathering long-term data from testers. Early mileage looks consistent through the first few hundred miles.
