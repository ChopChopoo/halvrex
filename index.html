Halvrex

A carbon rail that twists with your foot.

Halvrex is a small brand started in 2025 around one idea: a carbon plate that twists instead of locking flat. Most plated trainers use a stiff flat plate tuned for forward snap, which feels harsh on easy days. The HelixRail is a full-length helically-wound carbon rail: the carbon is laid in a slight spiral so it lets the foot pronate and rotate through the gait instead of fighting it. The result is a plated daily that stays soft on recovery runs and rolls rather than snaps. Halvrex is not out yet, but the first production run goes on sale to the public soon; for now it is only with a small group of testers. The line is a single daily trainer in three weights, all built on the same helically-wound rail.
