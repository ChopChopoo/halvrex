Halvrex

Stability without the hardware.

Halvrex was founded in 2021 to make stability shoes that don't shout about it. Most stability designs use a hard medial post that the runner notices on every step. The Halvrex SymFlow uses a softer density gradient: a gradual increase in firmness from the lateral to the medial side of the midsole, with no hard plastic insert. The result is stability that activates only when needed. The brand was started by two podiatrists who wanted a recommendation they could make without caveats. The line covers daily training and longer weekend runs for runners with mild to moderate overpronation.
